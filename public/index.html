MEGAHEX

Epic online multiplayer hex-tile turn-based war strategy

How to play
  • Capture cities with Infantry for +100g income per turn.
  • Build units at your HQ and cities. Terrain affects movement & defense.
  • Artillery strikes from afar but can't move & fire. Titans crush everything.
  • Win by capturing the enemy HQ or destroying everything they own.

Keyboard: arrows cursor · Enter select/confirm · N next unit · E end turn · +/− zoom · M mute · T chat · Esc cancel
